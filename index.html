About me

Hello everybody! I am Alina Sapronova!

I am from Moscow, Russia and speak both Russian and fluent English. I have been professional athlete all my life Cross Country Skiing and Biathlon. Sport has taught me to be purposeful and stress-resilient, to improve my skills everyday. When I finished my sports career, I worked as an assistant manager in retail where I learned communication and team work.

Over the past year, I have pursued a carrier in software development. I have completed courses that have allowed me to explore and throughly learn :FrondEnd: Javascript, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, GSAP, Figma, React.js and for BackEnd: Node.js, Express.js, AJAX, PostgresSQL, Git, Redux. As a former professional athlete I have the endurance, the purpose and the resilience to take on tasks and ensure that they are completed in a cost effective and timely manner.

I have U.S Work Authorization and hold EAD card or Form I-766
